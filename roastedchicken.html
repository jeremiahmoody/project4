Background

Roasted Chicken is one of the most popular foods in America. It has become a growing trend in the U.S. since the 1990s. Roast chicken is prepared by roasting in a kitchen, over a fire, etc. Roast chicken is roasted with its on fat and juices, which it is exposed to heat so these fats and juices is as "efficient as possible." Plus, roast chicken is a food that can go with a variety of meals.
